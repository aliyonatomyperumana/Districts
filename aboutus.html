About Kerala Tourism

Our mission is to showcase the natural beauty, rich heritage, and cultural diversity of Kerala — God's Own Country.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team dedicated to promoting Kerala's breathtaking landscapes, ancient traditions, and unique experiences. Our goal is to make it easier for travelers to explore the length and breadth of Kerala, from the serene backwaters to the misty hill stations.

Our Vision

To establish Kerala as the top travel destination in India by offering authentic and sustainable tourism experiences that benefit local communities.

Cultural Heritage

We celebrate Kerala’s rich traditions, festivals, and art forms like Kathakali and Theyyam.

Natural Beauty

From beaches to backwaters and forests to waterfalls, nature is our biggest treasure.

Eco-Tourism

We promote sustainable travel practices to protect Kerala’s biodiversity.
