Welcome to Kottayam

Known as the Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes, Kottayam is a blend of literacy, culture, and natural beauty.

History of Kottayam

Kottayam is renowned for being the first district in India to achieve 100% literacy. It is home to beautiful backwaters, rubber plantations, and scenic highlands. The district has played a major role in Kerala’s educational and literary history.

With attractions like Kumarakom, Vagamon, and Illikkal Kallu, Kottayam offers a perfect mix of culture, adventure, and relaxation. It’s also known for its vibrant Christian heritage and traditional architecture.
