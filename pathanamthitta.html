History of Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta, known as the 'Pilgrim Capital of Kerala', is famous for its religious tourism, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture. The district is home to the world-renowned Sabarimala temple, which attracts millions of devotees annually. Rich in rivers, forests, and wildlife, Pathanamthitta is also a paradise for nature lovers.

The region has historical significance with influences from Travancore royalty and various cultural traditions. It offers a blend of spirituality and adventure, from sacred shrines to eco-tourism destinations like Gavi and Konni.
